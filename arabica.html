% Arabica
376 Wukang Road
% Arabica's "net celebrity" attribute does not degrade its quality at all. Founder Kenneth Shoji traveled the world since he was young. His love for coffee determined his mind to make a cup of "world's top coffee". He owns a coffee plantation in Hawaii and dedicates to growing his own coffee beans. Using the bean’s name “arabica” directly for the name of the cafe also reflects his confidence in his coffee.
% Arabica has 12 cafe shops across Shanghai, all of which use Slayer Espresso coffee machines. Some have set up coffee roasting rooms, where you can closely watch the whole process, from roasting to grinding, of making a high quality cup of coffee.
